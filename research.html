Research Themes

Field Experiments

Long-term temporal responses of plant and microbial communities and carbon cycling to environmental change. We use field manipulations, including warming and precipitation experiments, to investigate ecosystem feedbacks and the dynamics of soil carbon processes.

Data Synthesis

Spatial responses of plant and soil carbon dynamics to environmental change. We integrate large-scale observational datasets, radiocarbon measurements, and global syntheses to quantify patterns and constrain ecosystem carbon dynamics.

Mechanistic Modeling

Mechanisms and uncertainties in ecosystem and soil carbon responses to climate change. We develop and evaluate process-based models to understand structural uncertainty, parameter sensitivity, and feedbacks in terrestrial carbon cycling.

Machine Learning

Combining process-based models with machine learning for mechanism identification and extrapolation. We explore hybrid modeling frameworks that integrate mechanistic understanding with data-driven approaches to improve prediction and generalization under novel environmental conditions.
